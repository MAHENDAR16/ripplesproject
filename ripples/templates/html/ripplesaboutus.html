Impulse is the official magazine of The Society of Electrical and Electronics Engineers(SEEE), College of Engineering, Guindy, Anna University. The EEE dept of CEG, considered one of the oldest departments in the college, embodies a ninety-two-year-old fraternity, with students being the cornerstone of it and capable of accomplishing any idea with integrity. Impulse is a completely student-run magazine. Our magazine enables the department fraternity to be engaged with technical stuff often. It serves as a platform for keeping the student community updated about the latest trends and upcoming technology, encouraging students to discover and learn, and instilling passion in budding engineers to explore various fields of interest!!
